## Allowed use

You may use the Zenlixem name to:

- refer to the original project

## Modified versions and forks

If you distribute a modified version or fork of Zenlixem, please make it clear that your version is modified and not the official Zenlixem release.

Do not use the Zenlixem name, logo, or branding in a way that could confuse users into believing your modified version is an official release unless you have permission.

Recommended wording for forks:

> Based on Zenlixem, with modifications.

Avoid wording such as:

> Official Zenlixem

unless the release is actually produced by the official project maintainer.
